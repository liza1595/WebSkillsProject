Skill Details


Graphic Design comes in many different shapes and forms. Some people specialize in advertising, some in logos, and other just in general art.
A common tools in graphic design are from the Adobe Suite, such as illustrator, photoshop, and after effects.
Typically for someone to get better at the skill, one has to master those programs or something similar to them.
In my journey of the skill, I initially just drew on paper for fun.
While being in college, I have taken multiple graphic classes including animation, digital design, and computer graphics.
Those classes have taught me basic artistic skills and the programs within the Adobe Suite.
Those classes along with personal and professional projects have slowly been building my graphic design skills
